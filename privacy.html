Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Wild Salmon game (the "App") handles user information.

Information We Collect

The App stores gameplay data (high score, coins, owned skins, settings) locally on the device. We do not collect or transmit personally identifiable information.

Advertising

The App may show advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data in accordance with its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Advertising is delivered in accordance with Google Play Families policies where applicable.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play listing for Wild Salmon.
